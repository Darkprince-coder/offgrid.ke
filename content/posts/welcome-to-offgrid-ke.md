## Kenya's Off-Grid Resource is Live

Welcome to OFFGRID.KE. This site exists to give every Kenyan homeowner, farmer, and small business owner free, accurate, Kenya-specific information about living and working off the grid.

Every guide on this site is free. No paywall, no sign-up required. We cover:

- Solar system sizing and installation
- Water storage, rainwater harvesting, and boreholes
- Food production and backyard farming
- Security systems that run on solar
